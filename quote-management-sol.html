#Case Study
Quote Management System

A veteran healthcare supply chain leader working with multiple institutions across
the US wanted to to reduce delays and costs in service procurement.

Solution

  • Using AppLegos platform, we developed an end to RFQ and Bid management solution that connected Healthcare providers to Service providers.

Implementation

  • Centralized platform for managing RFQs, bids and contracts.
  • Connecting Healthcare providers with Service providers via discovery and rating.
  • Automated workflows for faster processing and approval of RFQs and bids.
  • Real-time tracking and alerts of procurement activities and contract lifecycle.

Results

  • Streamlined the procurement process and reduced costs.
  • From conceptualization to go-live, the solution was delivered in a span of 4 months m.
  • Decreased development costs by 70%.
  • Improved visibility and control over procurement activities.
